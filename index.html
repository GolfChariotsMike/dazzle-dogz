What We Offer

Grooming Services

Every dog is unique — we tailor every groom to suit your pup's breed, coat, and personality.

✂️

Comfort Clip

All over length cut with a styled face. Includes bath, blowdry and nail clipping. Great for easy-maintenance coats.

🏆

Breed / Styled Groom

Breed-specific or custom styled groom — perfect for Schnauzers, Westies and other styled breeds. Includes bath, blowdry and nails.

💅

Tidy Up

Trimming of face, paws and sanitary area. Great for keeping things neat between full grooms.

🌊

Deshed

Specialist deshedding groom for double-coated breeds. Reduces shedding significantly and keeps the coat healthy.

🛁

Bath & Blowdry

Shampoo, condition, blowdry and thorough brush out. All services include nail clipping as standard.

🐾

All Sizes Welcome

From Toy (under 6kg) to Extra Large (15kg+) — we cater for all breeds and sizes with tailored pricing.

Our Story

A Boutique Salon Built on Love

Dazzle Dogz on Broadway is a boutique dog grooming salon nestled on Broadway in Crawley, Perth. We're passionate about making every dog feel pampered, safe, and gorgeous.

We believe grooming should be a positive experience — not stressful. That's why we take our time, use quality products, and treat every dog like our own.
