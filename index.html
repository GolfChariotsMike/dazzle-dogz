What We Offer

Grooming Services

Every dog is unique — we tailor every groom to suit your pup's breed, coat, and personality.

✂️

Full Groom

Bath, blow dry, brush out, breed-specific cut, nail trim, ear clean, and finishing spritz.

🛁

Bath & Brush

Shampoo, condition, blow dry, and thorough brush out. Great for maintenance between full grooms.

💅

Tidy Up

Trim around the face, paws, and hygiene areas. Perfect for keeping things neat between appointments.

🐾

Puppy's First Groom

A gentle introduction to grooming for puppies. We take it slow so they leave happy and tail-wagging.

🦴

Nail Trim

Quick and stress-free nail clip. Can be done as a standalone appointment.

👂

Ear Clean

Gentle ear cleaning to keep on top of hygiene and prevent infections.

Our Story

A Boutique Salon Built on Love

Dazzle Dogz on Broadway is a boutique dog grooming salon nestled on Broadway in Crawley, Perth. We're passionate about making every dog feel pampered, safe, and gorgeous.

We believe grooming should be a positive experience — not stressful. That's why we take our time, use quality products, and treat every dog like our own.
